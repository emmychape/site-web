Archipage

For this project i created an
edition on the Sanaa architecture agency,
with the architects Kazuyo Sejima
and Ryue Nishizawa.
For this edition i chose to transcribe
the duality between the two architects.

Postale cards and poster

These projects are a continuation of Archipage.
For the postcard, the goal was to
embed a letter with an image,
while translating the architects.
For, it's the same idea,
i added frames made in coding with
the Processing software.

Round 19

It's a wooden typography project,
in which you had to choose a wooden
typeface and from it you had to develop
your alphabet and then print your
wooden letters to create a poster.
